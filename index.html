The main problems in learning

Neither at school nor at the institute do they teach us how to study the material correctly. We are preparing for exams and learning tickets. We train ourselves to solve repetitive problems in order to pass the test better, but often in the end this does not give us real knowledge. Cramming quickly disappears and does not bring any benefit.

Conclusion:you also need to be able to learn, but for some reason this is not taught anywhere. What to do with it?

Specific techniques and exercises help to change the approach to learning, make it effective and exciting. The same techniques are applied on the example of training in the Practicum.

Teaching Techniques

Five practices from Barbara Oakley

  • Two kinds of attention

    Deep knowledge arises by alternating between focused and scattered thinking.

  • Recall

    Recall what you have learned — this will allow you to connect disparate portions of memory.

  • Interleaving

    Learn several skills at the same time, they enrich each other.

  • Questions

    As you listen to your teacher, come up with a good question that you really care about.

  • Illusion of competence

    Repeat to yourself, write it down, tell a friend: it just seems to you that you own a new topic.

For those who like to procrastinate

Barbara Oakley’s story

From childhood, Barbara was not given mathematics. She considered herself a complete humanist, and far from being the smartest. In the army, she studied Russian to get a raise, and so successfully that she was promoted to commander. But for promotion, you had to take math. And then Barbara came up with her own approach to the exact sciences. It turned out that if something is «badly given» to you, your knowledge gained by labor is much deeper than that of those to whom everything is clear at first sight.

Learning principles

by Josh Kaufman

  1. Choose an attractive project.
  2. Focus on one skill.
  3. Determine your target skill level.
  4. Break the skill into elements.
  5. Prepare everything you need for class.
  6. Remove barriers to practice.
  7. Set aside dedicated time for practice.
  8. Create fast feedback loops.
  9. Work out on a schedule, in short, intense intervals.
  10. Pay attention to quantity and speed.
